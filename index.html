-What is World Youth Meeting?

World Youth Meeting is held by Ritsumeikan University in Japan every year to provide students with opportunity to discuss about social issues and to take action for solving it, based on Sustainable Development Goals, SDGs.

This year's topic is SDGs number 16, "PEACE, JUSTICE AND STRONG INSTITUTIONS."

What is Child Abuse?

Child abuse is a problem in which adults hurt children through physical, psychological, neglectful, or sexual means, as defined by the Child Abuse Prevention Society.

Types of Child Abuse

PHYSICAL

・Sifting violence

・Causing physical pain

PSYCHOLOGICAL

・Ignoring the existence of children

・Scaring them

・Forcing them

NEGLECT

・Refusal to raise children

・leave children unattended

・Not sending them to school

SEXUALU

・Sexual assault coercion of sexual relations

・Giving sexual stimulation

・Subjects such as pornography

Current Situation

in Japan

In Japan, child abuse consultation cases reported to child consultation centers have increased over 11.5 times in the past 20 years.

-What is World Youth Meeting?

World Youth Meeting is held by Ritsumeikan University in Japan every year to provide students with opportunity to discuss about social issues and to take action for solving it, based on Sustainable Development Goals, SDGs.

This year's topic is SDGs number 16, "PEACE, JUSTICE AND STRONG INSTITUTIONS."

What is Child Abuse?

Child abuse is a problem in which adults hurt children through physical, psychological, neglectful, or sexual means, as defined by the Child Abuse Prevention Society.

Types of Child Abuse

PHYSICAL

・Sifting violence

・Causing physical pain

PSYCHOLOGICAL

・Ignoring the existence of children

・Scaring them

・Forcing them

NEGLECT

・Refusal to raise children

・leave children unattended

・Not sending them to school

SEXUALU

・Sexual assault coercion of sexual relations

・Giving sexual stimulation

・Subjects such as pornography

Current Situation

in Japan

In Japan, child abuse consultation cases reported to child consultation centers have increased over 11.5 times in the past 20 years. This may be due to greater awareness of child abuse, or changes in society. But sadly, some cases — especially those ending in death - might still be going unnoticed. graph: from nippon.com

in Taiwan

In 2023, a one-year-old boy nicknamed "Kai-Kai" from Taipei was rushed to the hospital with severe injuries-fractures, burns, malnutrition, and signs of extreme abuse. Sadly, despite medical efforts, he passed away. Investigations revealed that two licensed babysitters, the Liu sisters-referred by the Child Welfare League Foundation-were responsible. The case went to trial in 2025 under Taiwan's citizen judge system, drawing large public protests demanding harsh punishment. Public outrage led to over 110,000 signatures on a petition urging stricter laws. In response, the Legislative Yuan passed the "Kai-Kai Clause" on July 18, 2025, mandating life imprisonment or the death penalty for those who torture and kill children under seven.
